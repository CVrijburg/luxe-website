What to do in haarlem?

The old town is home to many traditional streets with restaurants, bars and specialty shops. Many of these issues are located in beautiful historic buildings with a special history. The Grand Place with the St. Bavo church is one of the attractions of Haarlem. Also definitely worth visiting'De Amsterdam Poort', the last remaining city gate in Haarlem, which dates back to 1355. Haarlem is traditionally a city of art and culture and in this respect there is still much to do. In the Frans Hals Museum is much to see art from the Golden Age. The Haarlem venues, the City Theatre Haarlem, Philharmonie, Toneelschuur Patronage and offer a high quality program in the field of theater, dance, film and music.
